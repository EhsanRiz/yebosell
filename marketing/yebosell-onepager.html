What your buyers experience
  1. Browse & order — they open your link, add items to a cart and check out with their name, phone and delivery choice.
  2. Agree & place — they accept the buyer terms and place the order, then get a tracking link to save to their phone.
  3. Track & chat — they follow the order status, chat with you, see your bank details, and can cancel before dispatch.
  4. Pay you directly — by bank EFT, EcoCash, M-Pesa or cash. No card fees, no middle-man holding the money.
