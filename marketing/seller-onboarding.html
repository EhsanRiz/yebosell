Seller OnboardingGet selling in ~15 minutes

Welcome — let's open your store.

Follow these seven steps on your phone. You'll need your phone number and a few product photos.

1
Create your account
Go to yebosell.co.za and tap Seller Login. Enter your phone number, type the SMS code we send you, then set a 4-digit PIN to unlock the app on your device.
2
Sign the agreement
Review the seller terms, type your name and place of signing, and tap Sign & Continue. A signed PDF downloads automatically — you can re-download it any time from Settings.
3
Set up your store
In Settings, add your business name, logo, pickup address, and your bank details for EFT (you can add more than one account).
4
Add your products
Tap Products → Add. Enter a name, price, photo and stock. Add sizes or colours and a prep time if you need them.
5
Share your store
Copy your store link and post it on WhatsApp, Facebook or Instagram. That link is your shop — anyone can browse and order.
6
Manage orders
When a buyer orders, you get a notification. Update the order status and message the buyer right inside the app.
7
Get paid
Buyers pay you directly (EFT or cash) — YeboSell never holds your money. Mark the order paid; the buyer follows everything from their tracking link.
Tips for your best week
  • Turn on notifications so you never miss an order
  • Keep your stock counts up to date
  • Reply to buyers quickly — speed builds trust
  • Use clear, bright product photos
