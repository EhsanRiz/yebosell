Welcome — let's open your store.

Follow these steps on your phone. You'll need your phone number and a few product photos.

1
Create your account
Go to yebosell.co.za, tap Seller Login, enter your phone number, type the SMS code, then set a 4-digit PIN to unlock the app.
2
Sign the seller agreement
Review the terms, type your name and place of signing, and tap Sign & Continue. A signed PDF is saved to your phone.
3
Set up your store
In Settings, add your business name, logo, pickup address, and how buyers pay you: bank (EFT), EcoCash, M-Pesa or cash — just enter your numbers/accounts.
4
Add your products
Tap Products → Add. Enter a name, price, photo and stock. Add sizes/colours and a prep time if you need them.

5
Share your store
Copy your store link and post it on WhatsApp, Facebook or Instagram. Anyone who opens it can browse and order — no app needed.
6
Manage orders & message buyers
When a buyer orders you get a notification. Update the status (New → Delivered) and message the buyer in-app, or send a ready-made template.
7
Get paid
Buyers pay you directly — bank EFT, EcoCash, M-Pesa or cash. YeboSell never holds your money. Tap Confirm payment to mark the order paid.
Tips for your best week
  • Turn on notifications so you never miss an order
  • Keep your stock counts up to date
  • Reply to buyers quickly — speed builds trust
  • Use clear, bright product photos
