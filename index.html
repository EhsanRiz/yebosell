How It Works

Live in 3 simple steps

No coding. No app to download. Just sign up, add your products, and share your store link on WhatsApp.

1

Create your store

Sign up with your phone number, choose a store name, and set your PIN. Your online store is live in under a minute.

yebosell.co.za/shop/?s=yourname
2

Add your products

Upload your products with photos, prices, and variants (sizes, colors). Set stock levels and organize by category. Manage everything from your phone.

3

Share & sell

Share your store link on WhatsApp, Facebook, or anywhere. Customers order online, you get notified instantly, and they get WhatsApp updates at every step.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about YeboSell.

Is YeboSell really free?

+

Yes — creating your store, listing products, and receiving orders is 100% free. We only charge a small platform fee per completed transaction, so you only pay when you make a sale.

Do I need to install an app?

+

No. YeboSell runs entirely in your browser — on your phone or computer. There is nothing to download. Your customers also browse and order from their browser, no app needed.

How do my customers get notified?

+

Customers receive automatic WhatsApp messages for order confirmations, payment reminders, delivery updates, and more — in both English and Sesotho. No manual messaging needed.

What payment methods are supported?

+

We support EFT (bank transfer), cash on delivery, and mobile money. Payments go directly to your bank account — YeboSell never holds your money.

Can I offer delivery and pickup?

+

Absolutely. You can enable any combination of customer pickup, self-delivery, or third-party courier. Set your own delivery fees — including free delivery above a certain order amount.

Which countries do you support?

+

YeboSell is built for South Africa and Lesotho, with local currency pricing (Maloti & Rands) and local payment methods. We plan to expand to more Southern African countries soon.

How long does it take to set up?

+

About 30 seconds. Enter your phone number, choose a PIN, set your store name — and you're live. Add products from your phone and start sharing your store link right away.

Can my customers track their orders?

+

Yes. Every order has a unique tracking link sent via WhatsApp. Customers can see real-time status updates — from confirmed to out for delivery to delivered — without needing an account.
