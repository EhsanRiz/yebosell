How It Works

Live in 3 simple steps

No coding. No app to download. Just sign up, add your products, and share your store link on WhatsApp.

1

Create your store

Sign up with your phone number, choose a store name, and set your PIN. Your online store is live in under a minute at selleros.co.za/shop/?s=yourname.

2

Add your products

Upload your products with photos, prices, and variants (sizes, colors). Set stock levels and organize by category. Manage everything from your phone.

3

Share & sell

Share your store link on WhatsApp, Facebook, or anywhere. Customers order online, you get notified instantly, and they get WhatsApp updates at every step.

Features

Everything a merchant needs

From product catalog to delivery tracking — one dashboard replaces scattered WhatsApp chats.

📦

Product Catalog

Add products with photos, prices, stock tracking, sizes, colors, and categories.

📋

Order Management

Track every order from new → confirmed → preparing → delivered with one-click status updates.

💬

WhatsApp Notifications

Buyers get automatic WhatsApp messages for order confirmations, status updates, and delivery alerts.

🚚

Delivery Management

Offer pickup, self-delivery, or courier. Set delivery fees. Buyers track orders in real-time.

💰

Payment Tracking

Track payments by EFT, cash, or mobile money. Record partial payments and send receipts via WhatsApp.

🏷️

Discount Codes

Create percentage or fixed-amount coupons with expiry dates, minimum orders, and usage limits.

📊

Sales Dashboard

See revenue trends, top products, customer insights, and order analytics at a glance.

🌍

Bilingual

Full support for English and Sesotho — every customer notification is sent in both languages.
